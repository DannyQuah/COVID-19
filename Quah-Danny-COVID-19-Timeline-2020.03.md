# Quah-Danny-COVID-19-Timeline-2020.03

COVID-19 Timeline 

by 

Danny Quah, began Mar 2020 (initially, with greater emphasis on Singapore)



| Date       | Event                                                        |
| ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 2019.12.30 | (Mon) Wuhan City government starts tracing cases [JHU][JHU-Data-Hubei-Timeline] |
| 2019.12.31 | ([Tue](https://www.who.int/emergencies/diseases/novel-coronavirus-2019/events-as-they-happen)) First report to World Health Organization China Country Office of cases of acute pneumonia of unknown cause in Wuhan ([WHO news release](https://www.who.int/csr/don/05-january-2020-pneumonia-of-unkown-cause-china/en/) - "WHO is closely monitoring the situation and is in close contact with national authorities in China.") |
| 2020.01.02 | ([Thu](https://www.moh.gov.sg/news-highlights/details/precautionary-measures-in-response-to-severe-pneumonia-cases-in-wuhan-china)) First Singapore MOH Advisory issued. Ministry monitoring cluster of severe pneumonia cases in Wuhan (Hubei province, China), with suspected cases to be referred to hospitals. Alert issued to medical practitioners, but no known cases in Singapore. Changi airport temperature screening for inbound travelers the following day. |
| 2020.01.09 | (Thu) Chinese Center for Disease Control and Prevention (CCDC) reported identifying a novel coronavirus &mdash; later called SARS-CoV2 &mdash; as  cause of the acute pneumonia. |
| 2020.01.13 | (Mon) China top officials secretly determined likely facing pandemic from novel coronavirus in Wuhan ([AP][AP-2020.04.15-China-Delayed-Six-Days-Pandemic-Warning]), but said nothing to the public. First case outside China recorded in Thailand ([AP][AP-2020.04.15-China-Delayed-Six-Days-Pandemic-Warning]). |
| 2020.01.17 | (Fri) Baibuting neighborhood, Wuhan, Lunar New Year banquet [JHU][JHU-Data-Hubei-Timeline]. |
| 2020.01.19 | (Sun) Respiratory specialist Nanshan ZHONG announced human-human transmission [JHU][JHU-Data-Hubei-Timeline]. |
| 2020.01.20 | ([Mon](https://www.moh.gov.sg/news-highlights/details/moh-steps-up-precautionary-measures-in-response-to-increase-in-cases-of-novel-coronavirus-pneumonia-in-wuhan)) MOH issued Advisory that people with pneumonia and who had traveled within last 14 days to Wuhan to be isolated in hospitals. China's President Xi Jin Ping warned the public that a pandemic was likely from a novel coronovirus ([AP][AP-2020.04.15-China-Delayed-Six-Days-Pandemic-Warning]) |
| 2020.01.23 | ([Thu](https://www.moh.gov.sg/news-highlights/details/confirmed-imported-case-of-novel-coronavirus-infection-in-singapore-multi-ministry-taskforce-ramps-up-precautionary-measures)) 1. First confirmed case in Singapore. CC\#1 (CNW, 66, arrived in Singapore with his family Mon 20 Jan 2020).  Had stayed at Shangri-La's Rasa Sentosa Resort; upon identification, warded at Singapore General Hospital. MOH contact tracing identified 9 close contacts who were his traveling companions; all placed in 14-day quarantine. Clear statement from Ministry: "MOH expects to see more suspect cases and imported cases. We urge the public to remain calm and vigilant, and to adopt good personal hygiene practices."<br />2. China imposed lockdown on Wuhan ([Lancet, 16 Mar 2020](<https://doi.org/10.1016/S0140-6736(20)30528-6>), [NYT 22 Jan 2020](https://www.nytimes.com/2020/01/22/world/asia/china-coronavirus-travel.html), [JHU][JHU-Data-Hubei-Timeline]) |
| 2020.01.24 | ([Fri](https://www.moh.gov.sg/news-highlights/details/two-more-cases-of-confirmed-imported-case-of-novel-coronavirus-infection-in-singapore)) CC numbers 2 (CNW, 53, arrived Singapore with her family Tue 21 Jan 2020); and 3 (CNW 37, son of CC\#1). CCs' detailed Singapore whereabouts traced and published. MOH undertaking contact tracing for close contacts. Temperature screening set up at [land](https://www.sgpc.gov.sg/media_releases/ica/press_release/P-20200124-1) and [sea](https://www.sgpc.gov.sg/media_releases/mpa/press_release/P-20200124-2) checkpoints. Advice issued against travel to Hubei Province; [Wuhan placed under quarantine][JHU-Data-Hubei-Timeline]. Lancet article by Chinese scientists. |
| 2020.01.26 | ([Sun](https://www.moh.gov.sg/news-highlights/details/fourth-confirmed-imported-case-of-wuhan-coronavirus-infection-in-singapore)) CC\#4 (CNW, 36, arrived Singapore with his family Wed 22 Jan 2020). As with others, asymptomatic on in-bound flight. Had stayed at Village Hotel Sentosa. |
| 2020.01.27 | ([Mon](https://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/nus-student-facility-latest-site-to-be-chosen-for-quarantine)) NUS Prince Georges Park Residence made Government Quarantine Facility |
| 2020.01.28 | ([Tue](https://www.moh.gov.sg/news-highlights/details/two-more-confirmed-imported-cases-of-wuhan-coronavirus-infection-in-singapore)) CC\#6 (CNW, 56, arrived with his family Sun 19 Jan 2020); CC\#7 (CNW, 37, arrived with family and friends Thu 23 Jan 2020). <br />A day earlier ([Mon](https://www.moh.gov.sg/news-highlights/details/fifth-confirmed-imported-case-of-wuhan-coronavirus-infection-in-singapore-27Jan)) CC\#5 (CNW, 56, arrived with her family Sat 18 Jan 2020). Travel locations in Singapore published. |
| 2020.01.29 | ([Wed](https://www.moh.gov.sg/news-highlights/details/three-more-confirmed-imported-cases-of-wuhan-coronavirus-infection-in-singapore)) CCs\#8-10 (all CNWs). Travel locations in Singapore published. No evidence of community spread. |
| 2020.01.30 | ([Thu](https://www.moh.gov.sg/news-highlights/details/three-more-confirmed-imported-cases-of-wuhan-coronavirus-infection-in-singapore-30Jan)) CCs\#11-12 (CNWs; 31, traveled with CC\#4; 37, arrived with her family Wed 22 Jan 2020). [Evacuation of Singaporeans from Wuhan](https://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/some-singaporeans-with-symptoms-of-virus-staying-behind-in-wuhan-even-as-92-are-evacuated).  // WHO declared a [Public Health Emergency of International Concern](https://www.who.int/emergencies/diseases/novel-coronavirus-2019/events-as-they-happen)  // [Duke-NUS](https://www.duke-nus.edu.sg/allnews/media-releases/wuhan-coronavirus) team cultured the coronavirus from an infected patient's sample, to aid new diagnostic methods, monitor potential mutation, and test potential vaccine. |
| 2020.01.31 | ([Fri](https://www.moh.gov.sg/news-highlights/details/three-more-confirmed-imported-cases-of-wuhan-coronavirus-infection-in-singapore-31-jan)) CC\#13 (CNW, 73, arrived Tue 21 Jan 2020). CC\#14 (Chinese national, 31, Singapore workpass holder, returned from Wuhan Sun 26 Jan 2020). CC\#15 (Singapore citizen, 47, traveled to Wuhan with family; evacuated along with other Singaporeans the day before, 30 Jan 2020). CC\#16 (CNW, 38, arrived Wed 22 Jan 2020). MOH: "The risk of infection from transient contact, such as on public transport or in public places, is assessed to be low." Total of 198 suspect cases tested: 16 positive, 35 pending, remaining 147 negative. Italian government suspended all flights between Italy and China; declared a state of emergency duration six months: The first EU member state to take such a precautionary measure. |
| 2020.02.01 | ([Sun](https://www.moh.gov.sg/news-highlights/details/two-more-confirmed-imported-cases-of-novel-coronavirus-infection-in-singapore)) CC\#17 (Singapore citizen, 47, evacuated from Wuhan 30 Jan). CC\#18 (CNW, arrived Wed 22 Jan 2020). For CCs\#1-18, MOH identified 245 close contacts, of whom 177 were still in Singapore and all but 5 had been contacted and were either quarantined or isolated. |
| 2020.02.04 | ([Tue](https://www.moh.gov.sg/news-highlights/details/confirmed-cases-of-local-transmission-of-novel-coronavirus-infection-in-singapore)) First local coronavirus transmission recorded. Of six additional cases, four (CCs\#19-21, 24) had not traveled recently to China.  Of these, three (CCs\#19, 21, 24) were traced to contact with recent travelers from Mainland China; the fourth (CC\#21) is a domestic worker for, and therefore a close contact, of CC\#19. The remaining two (CCs\#22-23) were among the Singaporeans evacuated from Wuhan Thu 30 Jan 2020.<br />Singapore's first local COVID-19 cluster at Yong Thai Hang ([ST 05 Feb 2020](https://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/coronavirus-after-2-cases-yong-thai-hang-health-products-shop-in-lavender-closed-as-more)) |
| 2020.02.05 | ([Wed](https://www.moe.gov.sg/news/press-releases/additional-precautionary-measures-against-the-2019-novel-coronavirus-infection)) Assemblies, large group and communal activities suspended at primary and secondary schools, junior college |
| 2020.02.07 | ([Fri](https://www.moh.gov.sg/news-highlights/details/risk-assessment-raised-to-dorscon-orange)) Singapore DORSCON Orange, indicating estimated moderate to high public health impact. Inessential large-scale events canceled or postponed. Personal contact (shaking hands, face-touching) discouraged. Self-isolation suggested for those with respiratory symptoms, if not yet tested negative.<br />Second COVID-19 cluster, Grand Hyatt Hotel. Conference 20-22 Jan 2020 ([ST 07 Feb 2020](https://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/coronavirus-gas-analysis-conference-at-grand-hyatt-singapore-hotel-linked-to-infections)) |
| 2020.02.08 | (Sat) Some empty supermarket shelves both then and the evening before. Document evidence? At [2000h](https://www.pmo.gov.sg/Newsroom/PM-Lee-Hsien-Loong-on-the-Novel-Coronavirus-nCoV-Situation-in-Singapore-on-8-February-2020) Singapore's PM Lee Hsien Loong addressed the nation |
| 2020.02.09 | (Sun) Calm restored in supermarket shopping. Document evidence? |
| 2020.02.11 | ([Tue](https://www.who.int/emergencies/diseases/novel-coronavirus-2019/technical-guidance/naming-the-coronavirus-disease-(covid-2019)-and-the-virus-that-causes-it)) WHO named the new coronavirus disease COVID-19 |

| 2020.02.17 | ([Mon](https://www.straitstimes.com/business/economy/singapore-downgrades-2020-economic-growth-forecast-to-05-15-on-coronavirus-impact)) MTI lowered GDP growth forecast to [-0.5%, 1.5%] |
| 2020.02.18 | ([Tue](https://www.singaporebudget.gov.sg/budget_2020)) Finance Minister Heng Swee Keat - Budget Speech 1400h. Unity Budget |
